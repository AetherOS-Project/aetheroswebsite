Installation & FAQ

A straightforward guide to getting AetherOS running on your phone
Expand Clean Flash Installation Instructions +
  1. Ensure your device's Bootloader is fully unlocked.
  2. Download the matching stable ROM zip package for your specific codename from our Devices Page.
  3. Reboot your phone into your custom recovery of choice (OrangeFox / PitchBlack / TWRP).
  4. Navigate to the Wipe menu and execute: Format Data by typing "yes".
  5. Transfer the downloaded ROM zip file onto your storage via MTP or OTG, then flash the zip.
  6. Once the installation successfully logs completion, hit Reboot System. Initial boot cycles can take up to 5 minutes.
Are Google Apps (GApps) pre-included? +
This depends entirely on the build variant for your specific device tree. Check the build labels or read the tags next to the build download button to see if it's a Vanilla (no GApps) or a GApps-included package. Updates are posted regularly on our Telegram channel.
How do I update to newer builds (Dirty Flash)? +
To upgrade without wiping your personal storage, download the fresh package update, boot directly into custom recovery, flash the zip archive over your active system partition, and reboot right away. Formatting data is not required for standard inline upgrades.
